COVID-19

It's stressful, but it is a once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Something like this will most likely never happen again in your lifetime, and it probably won't even take that much of it away from you. It's perfectly normal to be stressed or worried or scared for your family and friends, but you can't be freaking out about school or not being able to see your friends.

You live in a time where seeing your friends is easier than saying supercalafrageli... how do I spell that? Anyway. You can use social media to see your friends, and video chat with them, and talk. You can use games to connect with them and interact with them in a real way without truly being there.

Don't freak out about your schooling. The staff in your district are working tirelessly to find ways to keep students engaged and connected. You will be back in the game in no time. Your local government is also working hard to keep everyone safe. Some of these solutions aren't ideal for everyone, but they are put there to stop this thing. So, if you do your part and follow the guidelines, we will be out of this before you can spell that word I will not try to spell again.
