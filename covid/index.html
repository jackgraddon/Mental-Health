COVID-19

Tips to stay healthy, mentally and physically.

Take Care of your Body

  1. Wash your hands for 20 seconds or more.
  2. Eat well-balanced meals.
  3. Work out at home, or go for walks and runs outside if you can.
    4. A healthy diet and movement is great as long as you respect social distancing.
How to Stay Healthy

Take Care of your Mind

  1. Know you're not alone.
  2. Talk to your friends (FaceTime, Discord, Snapchat, etc).
  3. Manage time in front of a screen.
  4. Set long-term goals.
  5. Maintain a routine.
